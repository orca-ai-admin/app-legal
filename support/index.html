Frequently Asked Questions

Purchases & Subscriptions

How do I restore my purchases?
Open the app and go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. If the button doesn't appear, try signing out and back into your Apple ID in iOS Settings.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed entirely by Apple. To cancel:

  • Open iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions
  • Find the app subscription and tap Cancel Subscription
  • You'll retain access until the end of the current billing period

I was charged but can't access premium features.
Try restoring purchases (see above). If the issue persists, contact us at orca.ai.admin@gmail.com with your Apple ID email and we'll investigate.

Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund through Apple's official process.

Data & Privacy

What data does the app collect?
Only the data you choose to enter. All data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData / Core Data. No data is ever transmitted to external servers. See the app-specific privacy policy below for details.

Does the app use third-party analytics or tracking?
No. Our apps contain no third-party analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or behavioral tracking of any kind.

How do I delete my data?
Go to Settings within the app and use the data deletion option. Alternatively, deleting the app from your device permanently removes all associated data.

Is my data backed up to iCloud?
Only if you have enabled iCloud sync (where available). iCloud sync uses Apple's CloudKit and is governed by Apple's privacy policy. Orca AI does not have access to your iCloud data.

Technical Issues

The app is crashing or not loading.
Try these steps:

  • Force-quit the app and reopen it
  • Restart your device
  • Check for an app update in the App Store
  • If the issue persists, contact us with your device model and iOS version

My data disappeared after updating the app.
App updates should not affect your data. If you believe data was lost, contact us immediately at orca.ai.admin@gmail.com with your device details.
