Overview

Your privacy matters. Habit Stack is designed for habit building and daily routine tracking. All data you enter stays on your device. No data is ever transmitted to external servers.

Data Collected

The only data Habit Stack collects is what you choose to enter:

  • Logs, entries, and records you create
  • App settings and preferences you configure

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData / Core Data framework.

What We Do NOT Do

  • No external servers — Habit Stack has no backend and transmits no data
  • No third-party analytics — no SDKs, crash reporters, or performance trackers
  • No advertising — no ad networks or behavioral targeting
  • No account required — no sign-up, no email, works fully offline

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, your data may sync across your devices via Apple's CloudKit. This is handled entirely by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. Orca AI does not access your iCloud data.

Payments & Subscriptions

All in-app purchases and subscriptions are handled exclusively by Apple's App Store and StoreKit. Orca AI never sees or stores your payment information. Manage or cancel subscriptions anytime: iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Data Deletion

  • In-app: Go to Settings within Habit Stack to delete your data
  • Complete removal: Deleting the app permanently removes all data from your device

We cannot recover deleted data — we never had access to it.

Children's Privacy

Habit Stack is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will update the date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
