Overview

Your privacy is fundamental to IBSLog. This app is designed to help you with IBS symptom, diet, and trigger tracking. All data you enter stays on your device — always.

Data We Collect

The only data IBSLog collects is what you choose to enter into the app. This includes:

  • Logs, entries, and records you create within the app
  • App settings and preferences you configure
  • Health and wellness data you manually log in the app

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

Data Storage & Security

  • Local only: All app data is stored on your device using SwiftData
  • No cloud sync: Your data does not sync to any cloud service (including iCloud)
  • No remote servers: IBSLog does not have any backend servers or databases
  • No analytics: We do not use analytics tools, crash reporters, or performance trackers
  • No third-party SDKs: The app contains no third-party frameworks that collect data

HealthKit Integration HealthKit

IBSLog may request access to Apple HealthKit to read and/or write health data relevant to IBS symptom, diet, and trigger tracking. This is optional — the app functions without HealthKit access.

  • HealthKit data is accessed only with your explicit permission
  • Data read from HealthKit is used solely within the app to enhance your experience
  • We never upload HealthKit data to any server or share it with third parties
  • HealthKit data is not used for advertising or sold to data brokers
  • You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health

Payments & Subscriptions

If IBSLog offers in-app purchases or subscriptions, all transactions are handled exclusively through Apple's App Store and StoreKit. Orca AI never sees, stores, or processes your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all payment data.

Subscriptions are auto-renewable and can be managed or cancelled at any time in your iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

No Account Required

IBSLog requires no account, no registration, no email address, and no sign-in. The app works entirely offline and independently of any online service.

No Tracking or Advertising

We do not track you, advertise to you, or share your data with advertisers or data brokers. IBSLog contains no advertising frameworks, behavioral tracking, or cross-app tracking of any kind.

IBSLog will request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission only if required — and will function identically regardless of your choice.

Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • In-app deletion: Navigate to Settings within IBSLog to delete all your data
  • Full deletion: Deleting the app from your device permanently removes all associated data

We cannot recover deleted data, as we never had access to it in the first place.

Children's Privacy

IBSLog is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the app, please contact us — though as noted above, no data leaves the device.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the effective date above. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any updates.
